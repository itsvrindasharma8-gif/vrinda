Overview

From frustrating to frictionless

This project was born from a simple but damning observation: customers had to click 7 or 8 times just to find a product and reach its page. For a fast-moving e-commerce business, every unnecessary click is a potential exit. The research went beyond just navigation it covered everything that made the website harder to use, slower to load, and invisible to search engines.

The work spanned UX research, technical performance improvements, SEO infrastructure, Google visibility, and full platform builds across WordPress/WooCommerce and Shopify tackling customer experience from first impression to final purchase.

The fix involved a complete navigation architecture review collapsing redundant category layers, introducing smarter mega-menus with direct product family access, and surfacing search-first pathways for return users who knew what they wanted.

All Improvements

Six pillars of the overhaul

🖱️

Navigation Architecture

Restructured category trees and mega-menus to reduce clicks to product from 7–8 down to 4. Introduced predictive search and shortcut pathways for high-intent users.

⚡

LCP & Image Performance

Converted all product and banner images to WebP format, reducing average page weight significantly. Implemented lazy loading and preload hints for above-the-fold images.

🔍

Google Indexing & Crawlability

Submitted XML sitemaps, fixed crawl errors in Google Search Console, and ensured all key product and category pages were properly indexed. Resolved orphan pages and broken internal links.

📝

Meta Descriptions & Snippets

Rewrote all category and product-level meta descriptions for relevance and CTR. Added structured data (schema.org) for product listings enabling rich snippets in SERPs.

🛒

Google Merchant Centre

Listed the full product catalogue on Google Merchant Centre, unlocking Google Shopping placements. Resolved feed errors, mapped correct product attributes, and maintained feed health.

🎨

Graphics & Visual Quality

Improved product imagery standards and page visual hierarchy better hero banners, consistent lifestyle shots, and cleaner listing thumbnails to reduce bounce from poor first impressions.

Faster, Findable, and Friction-Free

The combined UX, performance, and SEO overhaul transformed the website from a slow, hard-to-navigate storefront into a streamlined customer experience that search engines could properly read and rank. Cutting the click path in half removed the single biggest source of user frustration, while the technical SEO work ensured the site's product inventory could be discovered through Google Search and Shopping.
