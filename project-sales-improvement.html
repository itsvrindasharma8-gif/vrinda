Overview

Doubling revenue from a standing start

When joining RedTape Limited, the e-commerce sales operation had untapped potential spread across five brand websites, 35,000+ SKUs, and multiple marketplace channels. The task was clear: use data to understand where revenue was actually coming from, identify what was holding growth back, and turn those findings into action fast.

Over the following year, a structured programme of SKU-level analysis, marketplace performance tracking, ad spend reallocation, and pricing intelligence drove a 105% year-on-year increase in total sales effectively more than doubling revenue from the baseline year.

Key Levers

What actually drove the growth

SKU Performance Analysis

Identified the top 20% of SKUs generating 80% of revenue. Deprioritised slow-movers, focused merchandising and ad budget on proven converters, and flagged discontinuation candidates using velocity and margin data.

Ad Spend Reallocation

Analysed ROAS by campaign, category, and platform. Shifted spend away from underperforming segments toward high-converting categories improving overall return on ad investment by reallocating the same budget more intelligently.

Pricing Intelligence

Built a competitive pricing dashboard tracking key competitors across platforms. Identified sweet spots where modest price adjustments maintained conversion while protecting margins particularly effective during festive season spikes.

Monthly Performance Dashboards

Created weekly and monthly Power BI dashboards surfacing sales velocity, channel contribution, and target tracking for the leadership team enabling faster decisions and consistent momentum toward growth targets.

How It Happened

A timeline of impact

Month 1–2

Baseline Audit

Pulled full historical sales data across all channels via SQL. Built a category-level P&L model in Excel and Power BI to understand where margin actually lived vs where revenue looked healthy on the surface.

Month 3–5

SKU Rationalisation & Campaign Restructure

Identified 300+ underperforming SKUs flagged for de-listing or bundling. Restructured ad campaigns around top-20 product families. Introduced A/B price testing on hero SKUs.

Month 6–9

Seasonal Forecasting & Stock Alignment

Built a 12-month sales forecast incorporating festive season patterns, category trends, and historical sell-through rates. Enabled the buying team to place stock positions with better confidence.

Month 10–12

Full-Year Review: +105% Growth Confirmed

End-of-year analysis confirmed total sales had grown by 105% against the prior year baseline. Revenue doubled with no increase in marketing budget every gain came from smarter data use.

+105% Sales Growth Within 12 Months

Starting from a clean audit of the product mix and channel performance, a structured data-led approach to SKU optimisation, ad spend allocation, and pricing intelligence drove total sales to more than double year-on-year achieved without increasing the overall marketing budget.
